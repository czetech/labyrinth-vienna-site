In this seminar **Mayu Shviro** leads participants through the sound, techniques and ornaments typical of modal music traditions. Working on bowed instruments such as cello, violin or viola (but also others like kemen√ße or kamanche, for those familiar), the class focuses on learning repertoire from different modal traditions, group playing, and improvisation in a modal context. The goal is to open up new sound worlds and ways of musical interaction beyond the classical-Western framework.  
  
**Student requirements**

*   Instrumental technique: intermediate
    
*   Western theory: any level
    
*   Modal music theory: basic
    
*   Sight reading: intermediate
    
*   Transposition: any level
    
*   Learning by ear: intermediate
    
*   Composition: any level
    
*   Improvisation: any level
